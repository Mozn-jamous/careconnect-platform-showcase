## 🏛️ Academic Context

CareConnect was developed as a comprehensive software-engineering project with full SRS documentation following **IEEE 830 standards** for requirements specification. The work demonstrates competence in:

- Multi-app architecture design
- Cross-app data synchronization
- Two-sided marketplace design
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Compliance-aware design (GDPR, CCPA)
- Comprehensive user research

---

## 🤝 Stakeholders & Research Participants

The four user personas that shaped the platform's design were validated through interviews and surveys with:

- **Working mothers** — primary research source for time-pressure and trust requirements
- **Non-working mothers** — feedback on self-care time and educational content needs
- **Babysitters** — input on income predictability, reputation, and safety
- **Mobile families** — needs around portability of care records

We deeply appreciate every person who shared their time and experiences to make this platform better.
